About Me

Dr. Sateesh Kumar Peddoju

Associate Professor & Researcher.

Dr.Sateesh is an experienced professor with over 25 years of teaching experience, including 12+ years at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IIT-R). He is a Senior professional Member of both the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). His research areas include CyberSecurity, Cloud Computing, and Pervasive Computing. He is also an Entrepreneur, Thinker, Listener, Advisor, and Motivator with a demonstrated history of working in higher education.

He is actively involved in various important services in the field of cybersecurity. He serves as the Communications Sub-group Chair for the IoT Security Workgroup under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MEITY), Government of India. Additionally, he is an Expert Member of the CERT-UK Committee, which focuses on CyberSecurity Guidelines, under the Department of Information Technology, Government of Uttarakhand.

He has made significant contributions to the academic community as the Founding Faculty Advisor of the ACM IITR Students Chapter and the Treasurer of the IEEE Roorkee Section. Sateesh is also a member of the Board of Studies (BoS) for multiple universities.With regard to his research publications, Sateesh has authored/co-authored numerous papers, including 63 research publications. He has co-authored a book published by Springer and edited eight books, six of which were published by Springer, one by IGI, and one by another publisher. His work has been published in various reputable journals such as IEEE TIFS, IEEE Potentials, and IEEE Access, among others. He has also contributed to over 35 conferences, including prestigious events like IEEE Mobicom, IEEE TrustCom, and IEEE MASS, as well as ICDCN, ISC, and CLUSTER.

Sateesh has been actively involved in research guidance, supervising and mentoring several students. He has successfully guided six Ph.D. candidates, with another eleven currently working under his supervision. Additionally, he has supervised the completion of 44 M.Tech projects, with two ongoing.

In terms of research projects, Sateesh has been successful in securing significant grants and cash flow, totaling INR 580 Lakhs (Indian Rupees). This includes INR 480 Lakhs in research grants as a Principal Investigator (PI) and Co-PI, as well as INR 100 Lakhs in consultancy grants. Sateesh has contributed to the professional community by serving in various capacities. He is the Founding Steering Committee Chair of the SLICE workshop and has taken on organizing, program, and web chair roles for IEEE MASS 2020, which is ranked as B. He has also served as the Publicity Chair for BDA 2022 and as the Program Chair for ICICC-22, ICCISC 2022, and ICRTC 2022. Additionally, he actively reviews papers for conferences such as IEEE TCC and IEEE TSC. His contributions have been recognized through awards, including the Best B.Tech Project Award and the Best DIC Internship Award.

Sateesh is also actively involved in administrative services at the institute. He serves as a member of the Institute Technical Committee, Department Academic Program Committee, Department Administrative Committee, and Department Purchase Committee. He also acts as the Faculty Advisor for M.Tech. (CSE) II Year and has recently served as the Chief Warden of NHVK.

Overall, Sateesh is a highly accomplished individual with extensive experience in teaching, research, professional services, and administrative roles. His expertise in the areas of Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, and Pervasive Computing, along with his contributions to various committees, workshops, and conferences, highlight his significant contributions to the field.
